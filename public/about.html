About me

Date of birth: 14.05.2002 Address: Kanzleistraße 2, 24943 Flensburg GERMANY Phone number: 07 07 07 07 07 E-mail: kimberly.eder@t-online.de

I´m a filmmaker, video editor, web designer and artist from Germany. I study media IT & film in Flensburg.

As a artist, I am deeply passionate about the art of visual storytelling. With each project, I strive to push creative boundaries and capture the essence of my subjects in a unique and compelling way. Collaboration with fellow filmmakers is a source of inspiration and growth, as we collectively bring our visions to life. Whether I'm crafting a narrative, composing shots, or editing in the studio, my goal is to create emotionally resonant films that engage and provoke thought. My journey as a filmmaker is a continuous exploration of the world's diverse stories, and I am excited to evolve and refine my skills as I pursue this lifelong passion.
